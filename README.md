## üí° Understanding "7 Doubles" - Illustration vs Methodology

**Important Clarification**: The "7 doubles" concept is frequently mentioned throughout the landing page, but it's crucial to understand what it is and what it isn't:

### What "7 Doubles" IS:
- ‚úÖ An **illustration of compounding power**
- ‚úÖ A way to reframe the $10K ‚Üí $500K goal as mathematically achievable
- ‚úÖ A conceptual framework showing that the "astronomical" target boils down to just 7 sequential doublings
- ‚úÖ A psychological tool to make the goal feel systematic rather than impossible

### What "7 Doubles" IS NOT:
- ‚ùå The trading methodology or strategy itself
- ‚ùå A guarantee that you'll achieve exactly 7 doubles
- ‚ùå A step-by-step trading plan
- ‚ùå A promise of specific returns

### The Actual Methodology:
The real trading strategy consists of:
- **7-tier risk scaling system** with buffer locks
- **Episodic pivot trading** (A, B, C trade types)
- **Disciplined risk management** (1-2% daily risk, weekly protocols)
- **Position sizing rules** based on account size
- **Buffer lock mechanisms** to protect accumulated capital

**Bottom Line**: "7 doubles" helps investors understand *why* the target is theoretically achievable (through compounding), while the 7-tier system with episodic pivot trades is *how* you attempt to achieve it.
